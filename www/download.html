Download/Installation

Intercooler is just another javascript library. It depends on JQuery 1.10 or higher being installed.

Here is an example installation:

  <script src="https://code.jquery.com/jquery-1.10.2.min.js"></script>
  <script src="/js/intercooler-0.0.1-alpha1.min.js"></script>

Versions

  • intercooler-0.0.1-prealpha-1.min.js
